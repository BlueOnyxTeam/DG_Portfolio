Custom Music composition D.A.W. In Unity

Functionally it worked, and had decent playback, and low memory footprint, and dynamic Audio effects options found in most DAW's. Solo, and mute options that could be controlled via code for Vertical layered composition. Worked similar to Kontak in that it had very compressed base audio sounds it referenced. Composition playback worked fine in Editor, and runtime as well. Ultimately the UI itself needed lots of QoL, and I had to move on to other work stuff. Was a very fun project though.
